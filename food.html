Food and Cuisine

Vietnamese cuisine is a symphony of flavors, textures, and aromas, reflecting the country's vibrant culture and history. Every dish tells a story, and every bite is a journey. Discover why Vietnamese food has captured the hearts of food enthusiasts around the globe.

Pho


The national dish, pho is a flavorful noodle soup made with aromatic broth, rice noodles, and various toppings like beef or chicken.

Banh Mi


A fusion of French and Vietnamese influences, this sandwich combines crispy baguette with fillings like grilled pork, pate, and pickled vegetables.

Fresh Spring Rolls (Goi Cuon)


Transparent rice paper wraps fresh ingredients such as shrimp, pork, vermicelli noodles, and herbs. Served with a rich peanut dipping sauce

Bun Cha


A Hanoi specialty featuring grilled pork, rice noodles, and herbs, served with a tangy dipping sauce.
